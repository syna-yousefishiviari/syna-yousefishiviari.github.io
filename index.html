Software Engineering Intern

May 2023 - Sep 2023
QUALCOMM

During my internship at Qualcomm, I had the enriching experience of contributing to the Software Verification team. I played a vital role in developing Nuvia's inaugural PC chip and the next-generation Snapdragon SoC for mobile and automotive applications. My responsibilities included creating Python scripts for parsing Lauterbach TRACE32 register dumps, developing a variety of manual and automated test cases across functional, setup, and performance domains, and collaborating with the DevOps team for CI/CD pipeline integration on GitLab. Utilizing the Parasoft Unit Testing Framework, I also crafted a Proof of Concept for the Power and Limits team’s firmware. My work on the inaugural Nuvia chip, a highlight of my internship, allowed me to contribute to a groundbreaking project, where I ensured the highest standards of quality and performance were met, leaving me with invaluable skills and experiences in the field of software engineering.
